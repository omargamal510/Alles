Alles Egypt

40 Years of experience in establishing readymade garments factories in different specialties in the Egyptian market through an exceptional group of international brands in this industry.

Our company is always keen to offer to the Egyptian market the latest technology in the industry.

In addition to that, the company cares to provide technical warranty for the different products through a team of highly trained technicians duly trained abroad at the suppliers factories & also though the collaboration between us & the producers in sending their technicians on regular basis to Egypt to preform training sessions to the technicians on the latest service & maintenance techniques, revise the technical performance at the customer factories and also emphasis on the technical warranty.
